Columbus, Ohio. Oct. 5, 2013 - 7pm - Centennial Park
Get ready for the biggest game of tag the city has seen.

Robots rule the earth...

Ever since the robot uprising in 20XX, humanity has been driven underground. Some just try to live undetected, but some have formed a human resistance, and hope to find a way to free humanity once again.

...A chance for escape

Rumour has it that the resistence has found the ruins of a spaceship, an old clunker from the early days of the space trade, and they've outfitted it to head into deep space, named it 'The Ark', and hope to use it to find a new home.

Gather your friends or run solo

There's a call going out to all humans, on all the broadcast networks that we have, hopefully uncompromised. The human resistence in Columbus plans to gather together on October 5, and make our way to the spaceship.

Try to make it through the night

Hide, sneak, and run from the robots, going through 5 different checkpoints throughout the Short North / Campus area. Meet fellow members of the Resistance, prove your humanity in different challenges, and secure your spot on the Ark, leaving earth.

Test your Metal.

We aren't so naive as to think the robots don't know of our plans. Or to think that all of the humans who start this trek will finish. The real and present danger of being assimilated into the robot army is unaviodable, but it's a risk you must take, as you journey through the night.
